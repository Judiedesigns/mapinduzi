Our Work

Revolution in action.

From research convenings to community co-creation sessions, capacity-building workshops to policy dialogues — here's a look at the people and moments behind our work.

Convenings Kenya

Regional Philanthropy Convening — East Africa

A multi-day convening bringing together 60+ funders, civil society leaders, and community representatives to co-design locally-led grant frameworks across East Africa.

2024
Research Sierra Leone

Participatory Research Design — SRHR Programs

Co-created a community-led research methodology for sexual and reproductive health programs, ensuring local women shaped both the questions and the findings.

2023
Governance Nigeria

Institutional Capacity Building — Women's Rights CSOs

A 12-month governance strengthening program for six civil society organisations, focused on financial management, board development, and resource mobilisation.

2023
Policy Mali

Evidence-to-Policy Brief — Local Development Fund

Translated field research into policy-ready briefs that reached three government ministries, informing the design of a new community-managed development fund.

2022
M&E Uganda

Adaptive M&E Framework — Self-Reliance Fund

Designed and embedded a real-time monitoring system for a $4M self-reliance fund, enabling grantees to pivot programs based on live community feedback.

2022
Tech4Dev Rwanda

Digital Empowerment Pilot — Rural Health Workers

Deployed open-source, low-connectivity data tools with 200 rural health workers — enabling real-time reporting without dependence on expensive proprietary platforms.

What We Do

A portfolio built for systemic change —
not just project delivery.

We offer a synergistic range of services designed to dismantle dependency and build genuine African self-reliance. Nothing here is off-the-shelf.

01

Evidence-Driven Insights & Strategic Research

Research

We specialize in participatory research — where communities co-create the questions, the methods, and the analysis. Because research that doesn't include the people it's about rarely changes the things that matter to them.

↗
02

Impact Measurement & Adaptive Learning (M&E)

M&E

We facilitate adaptive management, ensuring data isn't just collected but used in real time to pivot and improve programs. If something isn't working, we find out early — and we fix it together.

↗
03

Strategic Organisational Strengthening

Governance

Our governance and leadership development models are tailored to local cultural norms while upholding international standards of transparency. Stronger organizations build stronger communities.

↗
04

Community-Led Solutions & Project Realisation

Community

We provide technical backstopping — meaning we only step in when the community requests it. The power stays firmly in their hands, and we're there to support when it's needed, not to take over.

↗
05

Evidence-to-Action: Policy Influence & Advocacy

Policy

We use nuanced power analysis to navigate informal influence networks and cultural dynamics that global firms often miss. Research means nothing if it doesn't reach the people who can act on it.

↗
06

Tech4Dev: Strategic Digital Empowerment

Tech

We prioritize appropriate technology — open-source, low-cost tools that work in low-connectivity settings and local languages. Because digital transformation should work for everyone, not just those with reliable broadband.

↗
07

Impactful Convenings & Knowledge Systems

Knowledge

Our knowledge management and learning solutions prioritize African oral traditions and local narratives, ensuring vital institutional knowledge is never lost. Some of the most important expertise in the world has never been written down — we help change that.
